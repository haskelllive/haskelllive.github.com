Episode 1: The Chess Board

In this series we're going to build a chess engine from scratch. Today we will teach our engine a basic representation of chess pieces and boards, along with some helper functions to show them and read them.

About Haskell Live

Haskell Live is a free (as in beer), creative-commons attribution licensed series of live coding videos that teaches basic to intermediate Haskell concepts.
